Tovino

Tovino Thomas (born 21 January 1989) is an Indian actor and film producer who predominantly works in Malayalam films.[1] He made his debut in 2012 with the film Prabhuvinte Makkal. His breakthrough roles were in the films ABCD (2013), 7th Day (2014), Ennu Ninte Moideen (2015).
